# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the
Micelium Sigil community a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We pledge to act and interact
in ways that contribute to an open, inclusive, diverse, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

We expect all community members to:

- Be respectful and considerate in all communication.
- Welcome differing viewpoints and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.
- Show empathy toward other community members.

Unacceptable behavior includes any form of harassment, personal attacks,
publishing others' private information without consent, or other conduct that a
reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Our Standard: the Contributor Covenant

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its full code of
conduct. The complete text — including detailed standards, enforcement
guidelines, and the enforcement ladder — is available at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

By participating in this project you agree to abide by that document.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the project maintainers at **conduct@qwentrix.com**. All complaints
will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Project maintainers are
obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.
